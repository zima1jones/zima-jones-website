About Me

My name is Jordan Jones. I was born and raised in Baltimore, MD.

I'm 26 years old and in a past life I was a professional basketball player. Due to unforeseen circumstances out of my control that involved me breaking my foot twice, I began to seek other opportunities to make a living.

I always enjoyed working with young people and had the opportunity to become a teacher. Though I'm completely new to teaching, I'm enjoying not only my own growth but also the growth of the students I teach.

Having lived in Germany and about an hour north of New York City, I was able to see how public transportation is a vital part of a city's growth and development. This sparked a strong conviction in me to help bring that same development all over the world.

I am a firm believer in cities. I believe they should be living, breathing systems. Most importantly, I believe they should prioritize people over cars.

This is my website - read some of my thoughts, ask me questions, and let's connect. I'm always down for coffee or virtual chats. We need community now more than ever, so let's keep building.
